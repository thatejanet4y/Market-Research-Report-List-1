<p><p>Molecular scissors technology encompasses various gene-editing tools, notably Cas9, TALENs, MegaTALs, and ZFN. Cas9, derived from CRISPR systems, enables precise DNA alteration and is widely adopted for its efficiency. TALENs utilize customizable transcription activator-like effectors for targeted gene editing, while MegaTALs combine TALENs and homologous recombination for improved precision. Zinc Finger Nucleases (ZFN) employ engineered zinc finger proteins for specific gene modification. Together, these technologies are revolutionizing genetic research and therapeutic applications across biotechnology and medicine.</p></p>
