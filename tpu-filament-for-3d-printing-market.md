<p><strong>The TPU Filament for 3D Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester-based TPU</li><li>Polyether-based TPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TPU filament for 3D printing is categorized primarily into polyester-based and polyether-based types. Polyester-based TPU offers enhanced abrasion resistance and higher tensile strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability, such as automotive parts and industrial components. In contrast, polyether-based TPU is known for its flexibility and resistance to hydrolysis, making it ideal for water-resistant applications like footwear and soft touch products. Each type caters to specific user needs, influencing material choice based on performance requirements.</p></p>
