Its market is influenced by production methods such as the Cumene Process and the Ethylbenzene Process. The Cumene Process primarily involves the oxidation of cumene to produce phenol, which can further be converted to acetophenone. The Ethylbenzene Process focuses on the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to produce styrene, indirectly impacting acetophenone supply. Both processes affect pricing and availability in the market.</p></p>
