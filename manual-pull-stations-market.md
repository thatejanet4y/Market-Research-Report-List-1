<p><p>Manual pull stations are crucial components of fire alarm systems, allowing individuals to alert others in case of an emergency. The market can be divided into two main types: single action and dual action. Single action pull stations require only one step to activate the alarm, making them easy to use. In contrast, dual action stations necessitate two steps, often requiring the user to lift a cover before pulling the handle. Dual action is designed to reduce false alarms and enhance safety.</p></p>
