<p><ul><li>Batch Fryers</li><li>Continuous Fryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial frying machine market is primarily divided into two types: batch fryers and continuous fryers. Batch fryers operate by processing a specific quantity of food in a single cycle, offering flexibility in production and easy transition between different products. In contrast, continuous fryers are designed for high-volume production, allowing food to move through the frying process in a constant flow, resulting in increased efficiency and uniform cooking. Both types cater to diverse food processing needs in the industrial sector.</p></p>
