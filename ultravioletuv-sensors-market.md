<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) sensors market is segmented into three main types: UVA, UVB, and Others. UVA sensors primarily detect long-wave ultraviolet radiation, which is less harmful but can contribute to skin aging and certain health issues. UVB sensors measure medium-wave UV radiation, responsible for sunburn and more severe skin conditions. The "Others" category includes sensors for UVC radiation, often used for disinfection purposes, and specialized sensors for industrial or research applications. Each type serves distinct applications in health, safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
