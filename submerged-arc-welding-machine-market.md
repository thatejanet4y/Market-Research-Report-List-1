<p><p>Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) machines are specialized equipment designed to perform high-efficiency welding in various industrial applications. This process involves feeding a continuously fed electrode through a nozzle, which is submerged beneath a granular flux layer. This setup creates a stable arc and is particularly effective for thick materials, making it popular in sectors like shipbuilding, construction, and pipe manufacturing.</p><p>The Submerged Arc Welding Machine Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for robust welding solutions in heavy industries.
