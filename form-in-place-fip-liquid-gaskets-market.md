<p><p>The Form-in-Place (FIP) liquid gaskets market is segmented into two primary types: conductive and non-conductive gaskets. Conductive Form-in-Place gaskets are designed for applications requiring electrical conductivity, often used in electronic devices to prevent electromagnetic interference. Non-Conductive Form-in-Place gaskets, on the other hand, are used in applications where electrical insulation is necessary, providing a sealing solution without conducting electricity. Both types are valued for their customizability, ease of application, and ability to form precise seals in various industries.</p></p>
