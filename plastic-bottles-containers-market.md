<p><p>The plastic bottles and containers market is primarily classified into four types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). PET is widely used for beverage packaging due to its strength and clarity. PP is known for its versatility and resistance to chemical leaching, making it suitable for containers. HDPE offers durability and is commonly used for milk and detergent bottles. LDPE is flexible and often used for bags and squeezable bottles, providing lightweight solutions.</p></p>
