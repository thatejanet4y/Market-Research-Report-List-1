<p><p>The tea pods market is divided into two main types: soft tea pods and hard tea pods. Soft tea pods consist of biodegradable materials that allow for a smooth infusion while being environmentally friendly. They typically contain loose leaf or finely cut tea. In contrast, hard tea pods are made from plastic or metal and offer a sturdier design that retains freshness. These pods are ideal for single-serve machines, providing convenience and consistent flavor in each cup.</p></p>
