<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane sealants market is divided into two main types: single-component and multi-component sealants. Single-component sealants are prepackaged products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them easy to use and ideal for various applications. Multi-component sealants, on the other hand, consist of two or more components that must be mixed before application. These offer enhanced performance characteristics, such as improved adhesion and chemical resistance, making them suitable for more demanding environmental conditions.</p></p>
