<p><strong>The Run Flat Tires (Run-flat Tire) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting</li><li>Support Ring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Run-flat tires are designed to maintain functionality even after a puncture, enhancing safety and convenience. There are two primary types: self-supporting and support ring systems. Self-supporting run-flat tires feature reinforced sidewalls that can withstand the weight of the vehicle when air pressure is lost. In contrast, support ring systems use an additional support ring inside the tire to provide stability and allow for continued driving. Both types enable drivers to continue their journey temporarily, reducing the need for immediate tire changes.</p></p>
