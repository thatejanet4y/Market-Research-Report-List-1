<p><p>Thermocouple calibrators are essential tools used to ensure accurate temperature measurements by comparing the output of a thermocouple to a known reference. The market is segmented into two main types based on operation modes: Input Mode and Output Mode. </p><p>In Input Mode, the calibrator accepts input from the thermocouple and displays the corresponding temperature reading, facilitating direct comparisons and adjustments. Output Mode, on the other hand, generates a simulated thermocouple output signal to test and verify the accuracy of temperature measurement devices, ensuring reliability in various applications.</p></p>
