<p><p>The sigmoidoscope market is categorized into two main types: flexible sigmoidoscopes and rigid sigmoidoscopes. Flexible sigmoidoscopes are designed with a pliable, thin structure that allows easy navigation through the colon, making them ideal for diagnostic procedures and polyp removal with minimal discomfort to the patient. Rigid sigmoidoscopes, on the other hand, consist of a stiffer design intended for specific examinations and interventions in the lower intestine. Both types are essential for colorectal health assessments and treatments.</p></p>
