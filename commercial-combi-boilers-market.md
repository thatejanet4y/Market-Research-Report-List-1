<p><strong>The Commercial Combi Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-condensing Boilers</li><li>Condensing Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial combi boilers market consists of two main types: non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, releasing more heat through exhaust gases, making them less eco-friendly and cost-effective. In contrast, condensing boilers utilize advanced technology to recover and reuse heat from flue gases, achieving higher efficiency and lower emissions. This distinction influences purchase decisions, with businesses increasingly favoring condensing boilers for their energy savings and environmental benefits, despite higher initial costs.</p></p>
