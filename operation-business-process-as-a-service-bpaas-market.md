<p><p>Business Process as a Service (BPaaS) is a cloud-based service model that delivers business processes over the internet, allowing businesses to streamline operations and reduce costs. The market is classified into two main types: hosted and on-premise. Hosted BPaaS solutions are managed in the cloud, offering scalability and ease of access without significant infrastructure investment. In contrast, on-premise BPaaS involves running processes on local servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring more resources for maintenance and management.</p></p>
