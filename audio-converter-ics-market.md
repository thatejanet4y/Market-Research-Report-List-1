<p><p>The audio converter IC market is segmented into two primary types: audio A/D converter ICs and audio D/A converter ICs. Audio A/D converters transform analog audio signals into digital formats, facilitating the processing and storage of sound in digital devices. Conversely, audio D/A converters convert digital audio signals back into analog form, enabling playback through speakers or headphones. Both types are essential for various applications in consumer electronics, professional audio equipment, and telecommunications, driving advancements in audio quality and performance.</p></p>
