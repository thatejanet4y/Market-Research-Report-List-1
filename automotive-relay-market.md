<p><p>The automotive relay market is primarily segmented into two types: plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are designed for easy installation and replacement, featuring a standardized connector for quick connectivity, making them popular in various automotive applications. On the other hand, PCB relays are mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, offering compactness and reliability for space-constrained environments. Both types play vital roles in enhancing electrical control, safety, and efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
