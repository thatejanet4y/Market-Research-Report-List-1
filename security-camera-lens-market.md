<p><p>The security camera lens market comprises three main types: fixed, varifocal, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses offer a consistent field of view and are ideal for specific monitoring areas, providing simplicity and affordability. Varifocal lenses allow for adjustable focal lengths, enabling users to change the field of view as needed, making them versatile for various settings. Zoom lenses provide the ability to remotely adjust focal lengths, offering enhanced detail and flexibility in capturing distant subjects, suitable for dynamic surveillance environments.</p></p>
