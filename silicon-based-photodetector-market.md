<p><ul><li>Silicon Drift Detector (SDD)</li><li>Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based photodetector market comprises various technologies, primarily including Silicon Drift Detectors (SDD) and Silicon Photomultipliers (SiPM). SDDs utilize a unique structure that enhances energy resolution and is ideal for high-energy applications, benefiting fields like X-ray and particle detection. In contrast, SiPMs operate as highly sensitive photon detectors, excelling in low-light conditions and offering increased gain and timing resolution, making them suitable for medical imaging and radiation detection. Both technologies contribute significantly to advancements in photonic applications.</p></p>
