<p><p>The visible fiber laser market consists of two main types: continuous wave (CW) and pulsed lasers. Continuous wave lasers emit a steady beam of light, providing consistent power for applications like cutting and engraving materials. In contrast, pulsed lasers deliver energy in short bursts, allowing for high peak power and precision in applications such as marking and medical procedures. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and telecommunications, enhancing efficiency and precision in various tasks.</p></p>
