<p><ul><li>Spring-powered</li><li>Battery-powered</li><li>Gas-powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airsoft guns market is categorized into three primary types: spring-powered, battery-powered, and gas-powered. Spring-powered guns utilize a mechanical spring for propulsion, offering simplicity and reliability, ideal for beginners. Battery-powered guns, often known as AEGs (Automatic Electric Guns), use rechargeable batteries to power an electric motor, allowing for semi-automatic and automatic firing modes, favored for their fire rate and versatility. Gas-powered guns rely on compressed gas, providing realism in operation and firing performance, popular among experienced players seeking authenticity.</p></p>
