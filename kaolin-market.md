<p><ul><li>Hard Kaolin</li><li>Soft Kaolin</li><li>Sandy Kaolin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kaolin market is segmented into three main types: hard kaolin, soft kaolin, and sandy kaolin. Hard kaolin, known for its high purity and low shrink-swell properties, is commonly used in ceramics and porcelain. Soft kaolin, with finer particle size and higher plasticity, is ideal for paper coating and cosmetics. Sandy kaolin, characterized by its coarser texture, is primarily utilized in construction and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing diverse product performance.</p></p>
