<p><ul><li>Low Flow Delivery Devices</li><li>High Flow Delivery Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen delivery devices market is divided into two main types: low flow and high flow delivery devices. Low flow devices, such as nasal cannulas and simple face masks, provide supplemental oxygen at lower rates, allowing patients to breathe ambient air alongside the oxygen. High flow devices, like high-flow nasal cannulas and venturi masks, deliver a consistent and precise mixture of oxygen and air at higher rates, ensuring adequate oxygenation even in critically ill patients. Both types cater to various clinical needs.</p></p>
