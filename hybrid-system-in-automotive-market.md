Start-stop systems automatically turn off the engine when idling, reducing fuel consumption and emissions. Regenerative braking captures energy typically lost during braking, converting it into electrical power to recharge batteries. EV drive systems use electric motors for propulsion, offering zero emissions and improved efficiency. Together, these technologies create a more sustainable and efficient driving experience, appealing to eco-conscious consumers and regulatory standards.</p></p>
