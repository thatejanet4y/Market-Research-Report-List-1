<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody test market comprises various types, including Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs offer quick results and require minimal equipment, making them suitable for point-of-care settings. ELISA is a laboratory-based test that provides higher sensitivity and specificity for detecting antibodies. Neutralization Assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, providing insights into the immune response and potential vaccine efficacy. Each type serves distinct diagnostic and research needs.</p></p>
