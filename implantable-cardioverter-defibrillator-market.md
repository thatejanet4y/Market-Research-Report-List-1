<p><strong>The Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Chamber ICD</li><li>Dual-Chamber ICD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) market is primarily divided into two types: Single-Chamber ICDs and Dual-Chamber ICDs. Single-Chamber ICDs consist of one lead placed in either the right atrium or ventricle, targeting specific arrhythmias and suitable for patients with simpler cardiac conditions. In contrast, Dual-Chamber ICDs feature two leads, allowing for better coordination of heart rhythms by monitoring both atrial and ventricular activities, making them ideal for patients with more complex cardiac issues.</p></p>
