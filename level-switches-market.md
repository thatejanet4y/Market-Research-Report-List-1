<p><p>The Level Switches market comprises various types catering to diverse applications. Float Level Switches rely on buoyancy to indicate liquid levels, and Conductivity Level Switches operate based on electrical conductivity differences. Each type serves specific functions, enhancing efficiency in level measurement and control.</p></p>
